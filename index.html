Project Overview

Mental Wellbeing iOS App

MINDLINK Apr 2023 3 Week Sprint Lead UI Designer

MindLink is a mobile application designed to provide individuals struggling with mental health concerns access to tailored resources, support groups, therapists, and self-help materials. The aim of the app is to empower users by offering personalized assistance and fostering a supportive community, ultimately improving their mental well-being.

This project was a 3-week sprint where I assumed the role of the Lead UI Designer, however, I played a pivotal role in all aspects of the design process, from research and ideation to prototyping and testing.
